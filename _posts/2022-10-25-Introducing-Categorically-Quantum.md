Hey there!

Inspired by the fact that category theorist's love naturallity and that world is inherently quantum. I would like to bring to you Categorically Quantum, a blog that documents my varied readings and learnings.

The hope is that there will be a weekly post, along the lines of [This Week's finds by John Baez](https://math.ucr.edu/home/baez/TWF.html) briely describing some of the papers/books/concepts that I read and found interesting in that week.

Note that it's just an attempt at exposition, and any constructive feedback would be appreciated.
